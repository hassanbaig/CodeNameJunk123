About Us

Abdel, the Founder of Hanna Auto Mart, graduated from the University of Central Florida with a Master’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering but decided to follow his dream and passion of building his own business to help his community.

Hanna Auto Mart is been serving Florida for the past 15 years and have a great and successful years of experience in the Junk Car Removal business. Our service covers the area from the East Coast to West Coast of the Sunshine State (Florida).

Our management continuously works hard toward enhancing our services, with that being said we’ve conducted surveys to ask our loyal customers what we are missing to fill the gap and stay always in touch.

That’s when www.junkcartrader.com was born; it’s a website that allows our customers to reach us easily in an effort from us to make our service within the reach of a fingertip.

We want to serve everybody and all generations in Florida and make them focus on what is more important in their life than worrying about how to get rid of their junk car which is in the way.

The process is very simple and you can complete it in minutes, please go to our “How it works” page to learn about our process. Please like our Facebook page so we can keep you posted of what is going on.
